My Favorite Uplifting Music

"Here I Am, Lord"

"Here I Am, Lord", is a Christian hymn written by the American composer of Catholic liturgical music Dan Schutte in 1981. Its words are based on Isaiah 6:8 and 1 Samuel 3.

"You Raise Me Up"

"You Raise Me Up", is a song originally composed by Irish-Norwegian due Secret Garden. The music was written by Secret Garden's Rolf Løvland and the lyrics by Brendan Graham in 2002.
